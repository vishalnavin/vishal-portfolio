# About Vishal

Vishal Navin is a data scientist based in London, currently working as a **Data Analyst & Engineer at Accuria Ltd.**, where he builds data pipelines and AI-driven extraction that turn messy credit and securitisation data into something machines and analysts can use.

He has a strong foundation in computer science and analytics, and works end-to-end — from PySpark pipelines and feature engineering to predictive modelling, LLM-powered tools, and decision-ready visualisations. He cares about building solutions that are practical, explainable, and production-ready.

Before Accuria, he was a Data Scientist Intern at **Rockstar Games**, where he modelled player churn and retention on GTA Online telemetry, and an Associate Consultant at **Coalition Greenwich (CRISIL)**, where he built bank revenue forecasts.

He holds a **First-Class BSc in Computer Science** from the University of Leicester and an **MSc in Business Analytics (Merit)** from Imperial College London.

He is currently employed and open to hearing about interesting opportunities. Outside work, he's usually behind a camera or planning the next trip.
