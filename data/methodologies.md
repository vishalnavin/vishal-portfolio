# Methodologies & Approaches

- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** uncover patterns, anomalies, and correlations.
- **Predictive Modelling:** regression, boosting (Gradient Boosting, Random Forest), and clustering (K-Means) for segmentation and forecasting.
- **Data Engineering:** automated ETL pipelines (PySpark, Databricks), data-quality checks, and feature engineering.
- **Applied AI / LLMs:** retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and LLM-based extraction of structured data from unstructured documents (Excel, PDF).
- **Optimisation:** AMPL models for supply-chain and inventory optimisation.
- **Forecasting:** ARIMA and other time-series techniques.
- **Text Analytics / NLP:** consumer complaint analysis, topic modelling, and sentiment analysis.
- **Causal Modelling:** estimating the impact of interventions in business scenarios.
